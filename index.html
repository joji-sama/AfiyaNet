AfiyaNet

AI for Precision Livestock Farming in Nigeria

Empowering Nigerian Farmers with AI-Driven Livestock Care

AfiyaNet provides accessible, intelligent solutions to combat animal mortality and boost productivity for smallholder farmers, leveraging cutting-edge Llama AI.

The Challenge in Nigerian Livestock Farming

Nigerian smallholder livestock farmers face significant economic losses due to high animal mortality and morbidity from preventable diseases, suboptimal nutrition, and delayed access to veterinary expertise. This knowledge and access gap severely curtails productivity and impacts food security across the nation.

Our Solution: AfiyaNet's Llama-Powered Approach

AfiyaNet is a mobile-first, comprehensive platform designed to empower livestock farmers with AI-driven insights for enhanced animal health, optimized breeding, and improved nutrition management.

Local Language NLP (Llama)

Utilizes Llama to process voice and text inputs in major Nigerian languages (Hausa, Yoruba, Igbo, Pidgin), ensuring intuitive and accessible communication for all farmers.

Image-Assisted Diagnostics (Llama)

Llama-powered Computer Vision models analyze farmer-uploaded smartphone images for preliminary visual assessment of animal symptoms, enabling early detection.

Predictive Analytics (Llama)

Sophisticated ML algorithms, potentially based on Llama, analyze patterns in data to predict disease outbreaks and offer tailored feed optimization recommendations.

Affordable IoT Integration

Incorporates data from cost-effective IoT sensors (e.g., ambient temperature/humidity) to complement farmer observations and enhance AI accuracy.

Emergency Vet Connection

Facilitates quick connection to registered veterinarians or community animal health workers in urgent cases, improving response times.

User-Centric & Inclusive Design

Built with USSD, voice, and simple app interfaces to cater to varying digital literacy levels and infrastructural limitations in rural Nigeria.

Our Impact Vision: Next 12 Months

In the next year, AfiyaNet aims to demonstrate significant impact through our Lean MVP: we anticipate a reduction in animal mortality and morbidity among pilot farmers, improved farmer decision-making through accessible AI insights, and increased digital literacy. We will also validate our product-market fit and establish a network of community champions.
